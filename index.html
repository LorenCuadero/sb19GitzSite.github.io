ABOUT SB19

Know more about SB19

Talented Filipino Boy Band

SB19 is a sensational Filipino boy band known for their incredible talent, captivating performances, and meaningful advocacy for mental health. They have garnered a massive following and are celebrated as one of the most beloved and successful acts in the Philippines.

Hits After Hits

a testament to the remarkable success of the talented Filipino boy band, SB19. With each new release, they continue to deliver chart-topping songs that leave a lasting impact on their fans and the music industry. From catchy melodies to powerful lyrics, SB19 consistently impresses with their musical prowess and genuine passion for their craft.

Loved by Millions

a testament to the incredible journey of SB19, the Filipino boy band that has touched the hearts of millions worldwide. With their unparalleled talent, infectious charm, and heartfelt performances, SB19 has garnered a massive and devoted fanbase known as "A'TIN."

Passionate Fans

SB19's success goes hand in hand with the unwavering support of their passionate fans, lovingly known as "A'TIN." These dedicated fans have been the driving force behind the boy band's rise to fame, fiercely promoting and defending their music. Through social media, fan art, and fan-made projects, A'TIN constantly expresses their adoration for SB19, making them a powerful and united community.
